THE NUMBERS, EXPLAINED

Know exactly where your price comes from.

E1 Project Calculator Pro groups every cost into understandable categories, then calculates total production cost, cost per item, recommended selling price, profit, and margin.

MaterialsBlank products, color ink, white ink, varnish, packaging, and other per-item costs.
ProductionDesign, setup, finishing labor, E1 print time, machine use, and electricity.
Fixed job costsMaintenance, primer, cleaning cycles, and other costs charged once per project.
Quantity-aware mathSeparate the quantity ordered from the number of items covered by imported production values.

WHAT‑IF PRICING

The app gives its answer.
You remain in control.

The recommendation never changes while you experiment. Enter a selling price per item, apply a discount, or try a total order price. The app immediately recalculates total profit, profit per item, margin, and the difference from its recommendation.

APP RECOMMENDATION $805.50 $8.06 per item 45.0% margin
→
YOUR WHAT‑IF $1,900.00 $19.00 per item 76.7% margin

Example: 100-item order with $442.85 production cost.
